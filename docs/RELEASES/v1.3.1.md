# U4Claude v1.3.1

Cosmetic release — glass-tube look for the popover rings.

## What changed

- The 5-hour and 7-day rings in the popover now render as glowing glass tubes:
  - A linear gradient on the stroke (specular highlight at top → saturated body → ambient reflection at bottom) gives the body a translucent feel.
  - Two tight color-matched shadows keep the lit-from-within glow without the wide-radius "fog" of earlier iterations.
  - On macOS 26+, the rings are additionally wrapped in `.glassEffect(in:)` so they pick up the system Liquid Glass material. macOS 13–15 keep the gradient-only fallback.
- Ring color still tracks the live percentage — green at low utilization, yellow/orange in the middle, red as you approach the limit. The glow inherits whatever color the ring is at, so a 90% 7-day ring shows a hot magenta-red halo instead of a relaxed purple one.

## What didn't change

Everything else. Same APIs, same Sparkle integration, same data flow. v1.3.0 → v1.3.1 is a UI-only bump.
